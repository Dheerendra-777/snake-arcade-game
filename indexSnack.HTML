📖 How to Play & Controls

Objective: Eat the glowing food dots to grow your snake and increase your score. Avoid crashing into walls, obstacles, or your own tail!

🎮 Game Modes

  • Individual: Play solo. If you get a high score, you'll be added to the All-Time Top 5 Leaderboard!
  • Team Competition: Play with up to 10 friends. Players take turns one by one. The player with the highest score at the end of the round wins.

🕹️ Controls

Use up/down/left/right keys to move. To pause the game, press Spacebar.
For all players, both WASD and Arrow Keys work seamlessly during your turn!
